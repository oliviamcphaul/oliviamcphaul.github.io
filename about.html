Shallotte is...

Shallotte’s strategic central location makes it the central business hub of southern Brunswick County. The town’s population of 4,304 is deceiving as Shallotte is the spot where residents and visitors from miles around come to shop, dine and enjoy entertainment.

Big City Business

While you enjoy the sights and beauty of our small town, enjoy the shops and industry of a big city!Current anchor tenants in Shallotte include Belk, Tractor Supply, Chili’s, Golden Corral, Home Depot, Lowe’s Home Improvement, Office Depot, Big Lots, Walmart and Ashley Furniture.

Small Town Style

The town’s population of 4,304 is deceiving as Shallotte is the spot where residents and visitors from miles around come to shop, dine and enjoy entertainment.

Natural Beauty

Shallotte is home to a vital waterfront and near beautiful Holden Beach, Ocean Isle Beach and Sunset Beach.

Long History

The Town of Shallotte was incorporated on March 6, 1899, with a history beginning as early as 1734.

Family Fun

Shallotte has plenty of fun for the whole family, from our Market on Mulberry to our SummerFest celebration!
