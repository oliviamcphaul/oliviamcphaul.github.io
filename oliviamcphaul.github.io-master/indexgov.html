Welcome from the Mayor

Hello! My name is Walter Eccard, the Mayor of the Town of Shallotte. If you have visited us before, we thank you for your time with us and we hope to see you again soon! If you haven't had the opportunity before, we hope you'll come see us soon! Shallotte is a wonderful community, containing the industry of a city and the style of a small town all within our beautiful beach-side landscape. On behalf of the entire Alderman Council, Welcome to Shallotte!
